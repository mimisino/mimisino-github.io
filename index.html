Born Marvin Pentz Gay, Jr. in 1939

Started singing in church at 4 years old

Dropped out of high school to join the U.S. Airforce

After discharge, returned home to start singing career

Began at Motown records as a drummer

Tried to join the NFL when he was 31

After his death, his birthday April 2 became Marvin Gaye Day in D.C.
